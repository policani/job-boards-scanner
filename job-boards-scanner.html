Job Search Workbench

Local job search tool for filtering job-board results by posting age, location, pay, title terms, evidence terms, and exclusions.

Run Profile

Metro matching uses this editable city list. Use one city per line.

Default: remote roles in the United States. Metro areas load from a free local data file and use editable city lists.
For example, the filter uses the top end of a posted range. A job listed at $160,000-$240,000 appears when this is set to $220,000+.
Ranking priority: pay, capability evidence, then seniority. One day is the daily scan; use seven days when the window needs backfill.

Editable Criteria

Discovery and Routing

Use one title keyword or phrase per line. These are the strongest title matches for roles you want to find. Quotation marks are not required.

Use one related title word or phrase per line. These broaden discovery when the exact title varies across employers.

Use one seniority word or phrase per line. These are checked against the job title, such as Director, Principal, Lead, or Senior Manager.

Use one title keyword or phrase per line. Matching titles are removed before body-text evidence is considered.

Body Evidence

Use one keyword or phrase per line. These are searched in the job description to prove the role contains work you care about, not just a matching title.

Hard Stops and Exclusions

Use one keyword or phrase per line. If a phrase appears in the title, location, or job description, the role is removed as a hard stop.

Sources

Use one source per line. At least one source is required before a search can run. Supported formats are shown below; each platform has its own source identifier.
